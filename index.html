Spin Wheel

Team Size: Solo Participation Only

In this game, participants draw dares randomly from a bowl filled with challenges. The goal is to complete these dares using creativity and skill. The game typically consists of two rounds, each with dares of increasing complexity. Participants must confidently and exceptionally complete their dares to be considered winners. Success is judged based on how creatively and boldly participants tackle their assigned challenges.

Be Quiet

Team Size: Team of 3 Only

A team of three participants wears headphones with full sound and stands back to back.The host gives a random word to the first person.The first person communicates this word silently to the second teammate by lip-syncing.The second teammate observes the lip-sync and communicates the perceived word to the third person.The third person writes down the word they believe was communicated correctly.The team with the highest number of correctly identified words wins the game.
